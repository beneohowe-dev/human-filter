# AI Tell Filter

The filter looks for phrases and structures that make writing feel generic, synthetic or overly LinkedIn-shaped.

## Default banned and warning phrases

- in today's fast-paced world
- authentic
- unlock
- elevate
- leverage
- landscape
- game-changing
- this got me thinking
- here's the thing
- read that again
- let that sink in
- not just X, but Y
- properly

## Structural tells

- Overly neat question-answer blog structure
- Fake vulnerability
- Generic motivational endings
- Too many rhetorical questions
- Unnecessary em dashes
- Unnecessary hyphens
- Overly tidy three-part structures
- Abstract advice with no lived detail
- Fake question-answer blog phrasing

## Tone of warnings

Warnings should be useful and calm. Human Filter should not shame the user or pretend to detect AI with certainty.
