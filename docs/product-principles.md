# Product Principles

Human Filter is a human tone filter, not a content machine.

## Core job

Help people write with AI without sounding like AI.

## Product promise

The user should never have to remove the same AI habit twice.

Corrections, banned phrases, rejected wording and accepted drafts should become local preferences. Version one saves these locally. Future account-based storage should be optional and transparent.

## What matters

- Preserve the user's thought.
- Remove obvious AI and LinkedIn tells.
- Keep writing specific, believable and human.
- Make setup optional.
- Keep the product fast and uncluttered.
- Make warnings helpful, not scary.

## What to avoid

- A bloated dashboard.
- Forced login.
- Engagement farming.
- Generic AI polish.
- Fake confidence.
- Fake personal stories.
- A post scheduler disguised as a writing tool.
