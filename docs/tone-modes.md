# Tone Modes

Human Filter avoids the boring professional versus casual split.

## Modes

- Raw thought
- Less polished
- Curious storyteller
- Blunt but fair
- Warm but not soft
- Dry and understated
- Quietly confident
- More personal
- More direct
- Less LinkedIn
- Creative thinker
- Founder/operator
- Plain English
- Soft challenge
- Brave opinion

## Defaults

- Plain English
- Less LinkedIn
- Quietly confident

## Combining modes

Users can combine up to three modes. The agent should blend the modes instead of switching costumes.

## Guardrails

- More personal should not invent personal stories.
- Brave opinion should sharpen the thought, not fake controversy.
- Blunt but fair should reduce hedging without becoming cruel.
- Less polished should keep useful roughness, not leave the post messy.
